About me

I am currently an Assistant Research Scientist at the Department of Physics and Astronomy at the University of California, Irvine and a member of the ATLAS experiment at the Large Hadron Collider (LHC) at the European Organization for Nuclear Research (CERN) located near Geneva, Switzerland, where I do my research on the field of experimental high energy physics (HEP).

Among my research interests are Exotic and SUSY searches for physics beyond the Standard Model with leptonic final states. I have been involved in the Trigger and Data AcQuisition (TDAQ) system of the ATLAS detector with the emphasis on online trigger monitoring.
